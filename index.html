Our Mission

It's our mission to give people a memorable experience by providing them a world class hike!

Our team with over 100,000 hikes helps guide you along so every step is enjoyable

Our Services

From the time that you book the hike uptill the completion of the hike our team takes care of every last detail

We provide you with 2-3 team members (depending on difficulty of hike) that will guide, set up/take down camp and scout best routes

Our team is one of the best in the business! We look forward to see you very soon!

What We'll Provide

  • Hiking Backpack: Our team will provide hiking backpacks with necessary tools
  • Tents: We offer wide varrity of tents from 3-8 people
  • Sleeping Bags: Sleeping bags are provided upon request
  • Trekking Poles: Trekking Poles are provided if requested
  • Navigation Tools: Team members are skilled in navigation, so you are never lost
  • First-aid: Our team are CPR and First Aid Certified

Meet Rick
